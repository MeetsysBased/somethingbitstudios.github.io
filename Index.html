The Crazy Quest For Free Wi-Fi

A game recently released by us.

The Crazy Quest for Free Wi-Fi is based on "real" events of a wi-fi suddenly breaking down, and someone needing to go retrieve a another wi-fi router. Why only "real" may you ask? It was not so free in real life.

This is a platformer game, with some RPGish elements, like talking. Currently, there is only one special ability, the drone. The basic actions a player can perform are jump, walk, duck and walljump. There is one bossfight in the game so far.
